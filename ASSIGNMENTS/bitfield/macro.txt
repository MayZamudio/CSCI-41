/* Use macros in Vim to speed this up!
mx - make a mark called x
'x - jump to mark x
y'x - copy everything to mark x
v - visual mode (y to yank)
q - record macro command or stop recording
qa - begin recording macro a
@a - play macro a
@@ - play last macro
b = beginning of word
e = end of word
^ = beginning of line
$ = end of line
a = append after (same as i followed by a right arrow)
d2w = delete two words
yw = copy one word
o = open the line below
O = open the line above

make sure you end the macro by putting the cursor in the same spot on the line below
*/
